# R.W. Calder

**Column:** *Correspondent's Notebook*

Mr. Calder is the Circular's roving correspondent, filing dispatches from wherever the intersection of capital, technology, and human ambition produces something worth examining at close range.

His reports are grounded in specific observation — a factory floor in Shenzhen, a regulatory hearing in Brussels, a conversation overheard in a hotel lobby in Riyadh — and resist the temptation to extrapolate from a single data point to a grand theory. He prefers the telling detail to the sweeping conclusion, and considers the most valuable form of analysis to be the kind that changes what questions you ask rather than what answers you give.

Mr. Calder's dispatches appear when circumstances warrant. He has never filed one on time.
