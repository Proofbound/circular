About

Karam & Sprague’s Fortnightly Circular publishes longform analysis and occasional lighter fare on the intersections of technology, geopolitics, finance, and culture. Every piece is editorially directed by Sami J. Karam and Richard Sprague, researched with AI assistance, and held to the same standard of intellectual honesty we have always tried to maintain. The editors are responsible for every word published — including the words the machine drafted.
